Event Rules and Guidelines

All participants must carry a valid college ID card on the day of the event.

Each participant is allowed to register for a maximum of two events. Team size limits must be followed as specified for each competition.

Any form of malpractice or misconduct will lead to immediate disqualification. The decision of the event coordinators will be final and binding.

Participants are requested to report to the venue at least 30 minutes before the scheduled start time of the event.
